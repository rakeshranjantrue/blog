Places Must Visit

Santiniketan

I had visited Santiniketan in Jan 2020

Santiniketan is a neighbourhood of Bolpur town in Bolpur subdivision of Birbhum district in West Bengal, India, approximately 152 km north of Kolkata. It was established by Maharshi Devendranath Tagore, and later expanded by his son Rabindranath Tagore whose vision became what is now a university town with the creation of Visva-Bharati. Whenever you come to Bolpur, West Bengal must visit this place. Near the university Visva-Bharati there is a museuem and the house of Rabindranath Tagore. Most funny thig about this place is - There is a place named Deer Park but it has no deer. I mean in old times it used to be here. Now here is a playground. There are many robotic machines behind the university but now it doesn't works. It's there for showcase only. There is a very beautiful flower garden near the museuem.

Excellent film to culminate MCU Ride

Spider-Man 3 is a 2007 American superhero film based on the Marvel Comics character Spider-Man. This story is about an American teenager Peter Parker, a poor sickly orphan, is bitten by a radioactive spider. As a result of the bite, he gains superhuman strength, speed, and agility, along with the ability to cling to walls, turning him into Spider-Man. There are many different movies of Spider-Man like Spider-Man, Spider-Man 2, Spider-Man-Homecoming, Spider-Man : Far from home, The Amazing Spiderman, The Amazing Spiderman 2, Spider-Man : No way home and Spider-Man - Into Spider Verse.
